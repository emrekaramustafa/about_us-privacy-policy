1. Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Test Results: Vision test results and scores are stored locally on your device
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version (for compatibility purposes)

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it through the contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our eye test services
  • To analyze app usage and fix technical issues
  • To personalize your experience within the app
  • To send you important updates about the app (if you opt-in)

3. Data Storage and Security

All test results and personal data are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We do not transmit your test results to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share them (e.g., via PDF export).

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For ad display
  • Google Play Services / App Store: For in-app purchases
  • Analytics Services: For anonymous usage statistics

These services are subject to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access your test results and data at any time through the app
  • Correction: You can correct or update your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your test history and data from the app settings at any time
  • Data Portability: You can export your test results as PDF files
  • Right to Object: You can opt-out of data collection for analytics purposes

You can exercise these rights by using the app settings or by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. In case of significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email (if you have provided your email address).

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:
